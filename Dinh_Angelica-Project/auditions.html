Reefer Madness: The Musical– Auditions

Book by Kevin Murphy and Dan Studney Music by Dan Studney Lyrics by Kevin Murphy

Director: Steve MacKinnon Music Director: Spencer Crosswell Tuesday, July 24 7:00pm-11:00pm Call 407-841-0083 for appointment

Rehearsals are Sunday-Wednesday evenings. 7:00-11:00pm and begin on August 7th. Production runs from September 27 2012 – October 31 2012. (With a possible extension through November 3 2012) Please prepare 16-32 Bars of a Pop/Rock/or Contemporary Broadway song. Bring sheet music in the correct key; accompanist is provided. Please no acapella/cd’s/ipods/karaoke. Also please come prepared to dance. You may be asked to learn a brief combination. Please bring a picture & resume, stapled back-to-back. All roles are available, seeking all ethnicities, non-paid.
