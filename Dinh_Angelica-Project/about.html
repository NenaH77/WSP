About Us

Theatre Downtown opened its doors to the public in early 1989 with its 1st production of David Mamet's American Buffalo and we have not looked back since. Since the 1st production, its proven to be an invaluable cultural resource for Central Florida Community. Theatre Downtown was voted "Best Local Repertory Company" in the Orlando Weekly's Best of Orlando Promotion in 2001.
